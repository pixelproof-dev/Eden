About

About

Studio

PixelProof Labs is an independent indie game studio based in Potsdam, Germany. We focus on atmospheric survival games with deep systems and experimental narrative design. Currently building the Eden universe — a series of interconnected games exploring mutation, morality, and the collapse of corporate science.

Philosophy

Ship playable prototypes. Don't rebuild what works. Pseudocode before code. Visible progress every session. Structure beats motivation.
